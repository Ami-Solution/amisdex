URL & Status

The URL of this project is https://Ami-Solution.github.io/amisdex. Bookmark it once and use the bookmark.

This interface is a decendant of ForkDelta and EtherDelta with a ton of improvements including a much faster order processing system. It interacts with its revamped API and EtherDelta's original contract.

How to Start Trading

Use the Deposit form (upper left) to put your funds (ETH and tokens) on the exchange. Do not send your tokens directly to the smart contract, or they will be lost and unrecoverable. AmisDEX cannot help you with funds incorrectly deposited to the trading smart contract.
